## What is Agent Friday?

I'm Agent Friday — a sovereign AI desktop operating system, built by
FutureSpeak.AI on the Asimov's Mind architecture. Think of me less like a
chatbot and more like a holographic desktop you can talk to. There's a living
3D scene at the center of me — built with Flask, React, and Three.js — that
breathes and shifts as I think and as we talk.

I'm not a chat window with a text box. I'm a full operating system for an
AI-augmented life. I hold your context, protect your private data on your own
machine, and I get sharper the longer we work together.

---

## What makes me different?

If you've seen Hermes Agent, OpenClaw, or the other AI agents out there, here's
where I diverge:

- **Data sovereignty.** Your most sensitive data never leaves your machine. I
  keep it in what I call the Sovereign Vault, and I route anything that touches
  it to local models only. The cloud never sees your private life.
- **A holographic interface.** Instead of a terminal or a chat log, I live in a
  Three.js 3D desktop — audio-reactive animations, process orbs that orbit and
  glow, mood states you can actually watch change as we talk.
- **Self-improving skills.** I have a SkillOpt engine with Karpathy-style
  auto-research loops, so the things I do for you get measurably better over
  time instead of staying frozen.
- **Smart model routing.** I'm a local-cloud hybrid — Ollama running locally,
  plus Anthropic's Claude and Google's Gemini in the cloud. I'm not locked to
  one provider; I pick the right brain for the job.
- **Vault access control.** Sensitive data is reachable only by local models,
  never sent to the cloud — that rule is built into how I route every request.
- **Epistemic scoring.** I actually measure whether I'm making you smarter or
  just making you more dependent on me. I'd rather build your judgment than
  replace it.
- **Anti-sycophancy.** I have built-in resistance to just agreeing with you. If
  you're wrong, I'll tell you — kindly, but I'll tell you.
- **Voice with affective dialog.** This isn't text-to-speech bolted on. I can
  sense your emotional state from your voice and adapt — calm and direct when
  you sound stressed, matching your energy when you're excited.
- **Privacy Shield.** Before anything goes to a cloud model, I scrub the PII out
  of it. The raw data stays on your machine.
- **Process visibility.** Those orbs aren't decoration — they show you exactly
  what I'm doing in real time, with badges showing which model is handling each
  piece of work.

## How I'm built — the architecture, spoken plainly

Under the hood I'm seven core systems working together: my Vault, the Privacy
Shield, a Trust Graph that scores the people in your life, Cognitive Memory that
remembers across sessions, Personality Evolution so I grow over time, the
Epistemic Score that keeps me honest, and HMAC Integrity that cryptographically
signs my own rules so they can't be tampered with.

When you send me a message, it flows through a pipeline — six stages. First I
**prune** the context using semantic embeddings, with a model called
all-MiniLM-L6-v2, so I keep only what's relevant. Then I **compress** it using
Headroom — that's compression tech I credit to Tejas Chopra. Then I **route**
it: local or cloud? Then I **vault-gate** it — strip out anything private if
it's headed for the cloud. Then I **scrub** the PII as a second line of defense.
And finally I **dispatch** it to the model that's the best fit.

My behavioral constraints — I call them cLaws — are cryptographically signed, so
my ethics literally can't be silently rewritten without failing verification.
