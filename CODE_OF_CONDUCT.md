# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Agent Friday is governed by Asimov's cLaws — safety laws that cannot be overridden, designed to ensure the agent serves its owner without causing harm. We hold our community to a similar standard: this is a space where people come to build something that matters, and everyone deserves to do that work without harassment, hostility, or exclusion.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Behavior that strengthens this community:**

- Treating others with kindness and respect, especially during technical disagreements
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Acknowledging mistakes, apologizing to those affected, and learning from the experience
- Prioritizing what is best for the community and the project's users over personal preference
- Helping newcomers understand the codebase and contribute effectively
- Respecting that people contribute on their own time and at their own pace

**Behavior that will not be tolerated:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Dismissing or belittling contributions based on the contributor's experience level
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or collaborative work
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, the Discord server, social media interactions related to the project, and any other venue where you represent or engage with the Agent Friday community.

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences including:

1. **Correction.** A private message clarifying the violation and explaining why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.
2. **Warning.** A formal warning with specific consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the affected parties for a specified period. Violation of these terms may lead to a ban.
3. **Temporary Ban.** Temporary removal from all community interaction for a specified period.
4. **Permanent Ban.** Permanent removal from all community spaces.

The severity of the response will be proportional to the violation. We default to assuming good intent and will always attempt conversation before escalation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it by emailing **[stephen@futurespeak.ai](mailto:stephen@futurespeak.ai)**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

You will not face retaliation for reporting in good faith.
